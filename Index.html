My favorite recipe: Pumpkin Cheesecake

Pumpkin Cheesecke Ingredients

  • Graham Crackers
  • Cream Cheese
  • Pumpkin Puree
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Pumpkin Pie Spice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven, Prepair pan, Get crust
  2. Press crust in pan
  3. Bake crust
  4. Mix cream cheese filling
  5. Add flour
  6. Add pumpkin and spices
  7. Add eggs
  8. Assemble cheesecake
  9. Bake cheesecke
  10. Cool,Refrigerate,and serve
